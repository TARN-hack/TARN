About

30 seconds from your next play.

As short-form video content rises in popularity due to apps like TikTok and Triller, social media is shifting from just pictures and text-based apps. Short-form video content should eventually be something professional platforms consider implementing as traditional modes of job searching have become more digital.

How might we do this?

We are all familiar with the brief speech that happens within 20 to 30 seconds in professional environments called an elevator pitch. An elevator pitch is a simple and direct opportunity for job seekers to share their skills more engagingly. Pitch would be a short-form video platform for recruiters to seek out potential employees aside from viewing just resumes and profiles.
